Frequently Asked Questions


Can I login without a Google Account?

No!

You can access our services only through a Google Account. Why? Because virtually everyone has a Google account and this way you don't have to remember yet another password. Ok, also because we are huge Google fans.

Don't worry, the login operation is completely managed by Google, Wokhei does not have access to your password or any other personal info!


Why there is only one revision?

Traditional revisions are not part of the Wokhei workflow.

If you don't like your logo after the free revision we offer (unlikely but possible) just place another order and cross your fingers. Or walk away - no hard feelings - we are aware Wokhei may not be the service for just everyone.


What's the story with the upfront micro-payment? What am I buying?

You will agree with us that we need to make sure you really need a logo before we allocate the work to our designers (otherwise we'd end up doing logos for all sorts of bums out of the street!).
The micro-payment is just a symbolic token to show us your commitment - you're buying the effort from our designers to come up with a proposal based on your logo request. Also the amount of the tip is entirely up to you and after the micro-payment you have no further obligation towards Wokhei.
Also the amount you decide to transfer up-front will be subtracted from the total should you decide to buy!


Am I obliged to buy the logo?

No! There is no buying obligation, we don't take any money if you don't like your logo (exception made for the symbolic tip required to place the order). That's what makes Wokhei so special!
If you like you logo and decide to buy it, the amount you transferred to us in the micro-payment will be subtracted from the total.
You will not get back the micro-payment money though should you decide not to buy (we'll use it to buy candy for the poor designer whose heart you broke).


When can I download my logo?

You will be able to download both rasterized and vectorial version of the logo right after the final payment.
Should you decide to steal the logo with a cheap print screen, prepare for the worst. We will take it personally and put a bounty hunter on your tracks!


Why should I use Wokhei rather than any other logo design service?

Fair question!
The official answer is: because we've got unique designers! Just browse through our interactive slide-show on the homepage.
In case you didn't like the official answer, here's another one: because there's no obligation to buy!
The only thing we ask of you is a symbolic micro-payment upfront, to make sure you really need a logo. After that, your logo will be ready in 24 hrs, you can suggest a few changes as part of your free revision and buy it if you love it or walk away if you don't - again, no hard feelings.
In a nutshell: only a minimal upfront micro-payment, one free revision, clockwork 24 + 24 hrs service and no buying obligation!


How can I pay for my logo?

You can easily pay through Paypal either using your Paypal account or directly via all majors credit cards.


If I don't like my logo after the free revision, can I request the exact same logo?

Yes - but you'll have to deposit a symbolic micro-payment for each logo request.


Is Wokhei in any way affiliated with Google?

No. Wokhei is powered by the Google App Engine and the Google Web Toolkit but is not affiliated with Google.
